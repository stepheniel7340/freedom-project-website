INFO: What is Fermentation?

Fermentation is a chemical process, where molecules, glucose, bacteria, yeast are broken down. Oxygen is needed for many living organisms, but in this process oxygen is excluded. Many types of fermented food that we eat are cheese, yogurt, sourdoughbread, kimchi, sauerkraut, the list goes on.

There are 3 main types of Fermentation

Lactic Acid Fermentation, Ethyl Alcohol Fermentation, Acetic Acid Fermentation
