Acetic Acid Fermentation

Chemical Formula: C2H5OH + O2 → CH3COOH + H2O

Acetic Acid Fermentation is produced from the oxidation of Ethanol Fermentation or Alcohol Fermentation. It’s mainly used to make vinegar.

The fermentation process is built off of the same process as Fermentation or Alcohol Fermentation, the end result with Acetic Acid molecules.
